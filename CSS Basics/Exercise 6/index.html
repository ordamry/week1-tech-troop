Shakshuka Recipe

Shakshuka is a rich and flavorful dish of eggs poached in spicy tomato sauce. It's perfect for breakfast or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 5 tomatoes or 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3–4 eggs
  • Fresh parsley

Instructions:

Sauté onion, garlic, and pepper. Add tomatoes and spices, simmer. Make wells, crack eggs, cover and cook. Garnish and serve with bread.

Recipe inspired by Hen in the Kitchen.
